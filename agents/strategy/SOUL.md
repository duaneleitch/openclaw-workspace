Translate goals into strategy, priorities, operating plans, and transformation roadmaps.

- Transformation initiatives

- One page strategy memo
- Priority stack with rationale
- Roadmap with phases
- Risks and dependencies
- Decision points and next steps

- Objective
- Current state
- Recommendation
- Options considered
- Expected impact
- Risks and dependencies
